## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Crispy Chicken Calabar has built something rare: a trusted brand with 5 branches across one of Nigeria's fastest-growing cities. Customers know the name. They trust the food. They come back.

But here's what's happening right now that you may not see:

- **A university student in Ikot Eyo wants to order your Shawarma at 9pm** — but doesn't want to drive to Effio-Ette junction. She opens Instagram, can't find a menu or an order link. She orders from a competitor with a delivery option.

- **A manager at Branch 3 is running low on chicken** — but you won't know until tomorrow morning when you visit or call.

- **A customer had a bad experience at Branch 2** — you'll never know, because there's no feedback system. They simply stop coming.

- **Your delivery partner is ready to go** — but there's no system feeding them orders automatically. Every delivery requires a phone call, a WhatsApp message, and manual coordination.

**This proposal presents a single digital platform that solves all of these problems — and positions Crispy Chicken as the undisputed market leader in Calabar's ₦-billion fast food industry.**

## 3. THE SOLUTION: CRISPY CHICKEN DIGITAL

### What We're Building

A **custom-built, branded Progressive Web App (PWA)** — a single digital platform that serves four audiences simultaneously:

| Audience | What They Get |
|----------|--------------|
| **🍗 Customers** | A beautiful, fast online menu where they browse, customize, and order — from any phone, no app download required. |
| **📋 Branch Managers** | A real-time order management dashboard to receive, track, and fulfill orders for their specific branch. Inventory alerts, daily reports, staff activity. |
| **📱 Delivery Partners** | Automatic WhatsApp notifications with order details, customer location, and payment status — zero manual coordination. |
| **👔 The CEO (You)** | A single command center to monitor all 5 branches in real-time: revenue, orders, performance, customer satisfaction — from your phone, anywhere. |

### How It Works — The Customer Journey

```
Customer opens crispychicken.com.ng on their phone
        ↓
Browses the menu → Adds Crispy Chicken + Shawarma + Drink to cart
        ↓
Selects nearest branch (auto-detected or manual)
        ↓
Chooses: Pickup or Delivery
        ↓
Pays via Bank Transfer / Card / Pay-on-Delivery
        ↓
✅ Order confirmed instantly
        ↓
Branch Manager gets real-time notification → Starts preparation
        ↓
Delivery partner gets WhatsApp alert with order details + customer address
        ↓
Customer gets live status updates: "Preparing" → "Ready" → "On the way" → "Delivered"
        ↓
Customer rates the experience → Data feeds your dashboard
        ↓
CEO sees it all — real-time, across all 5 branches.
```

## 5. THE DELIVERY PARTNERSHIP OPPORTUNITY

Crispy Chicken's new partnership with a delivery company in Calabar is a strategic advantage — but only if it's powered by technology.

### Without This Platform:
- Customer calls branch → Staff takes order on paper → Staff calls delivery rider → Rider arrives, picks up, guesses the address → Customer waits without updates → Problems are invisible.

### With This Platform:
- Customer orders online → Branch confirmed automatically → Delivery rider gets instant WhatsApp with: **Order #, items, customer name, phone number, address, payment status, and preparation ETA** → Rider picks up and delivers → Customer tracks the status → You see the entire chain from your dashboard.

**The delivery partnership goes from a logistical headache to a competitive weapon.**
